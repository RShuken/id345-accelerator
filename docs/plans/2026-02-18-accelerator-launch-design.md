## Context

ID345 is an AI hacker space / co-working space in Denver running popular weekly Wednesday meetups alternating between AI coding and AI generative media/art. The community has grown to 3+ chapters and includes startup founders, mentors, newbies, and non-coders building incredible things with AI.

ID345 is launching **Colorado's first AI accelerator program**.

### The Space Aesthetic
Retro-tech hacker space: CRT TVs, ham radios, old Apple computers, pinball machines, colorful eclectic furniture, plants. Fun, creative, authentic.

## Program Structure

- **Name**: ID345 Accelerator
- **Duration**: ~6 weeks, mostly remote
- **In-person**: Weekly events at the space + Demo Day finale
- **Cohort size**: 10-15 companies
- **Investment model**: SAFE notes (preferred, final structure TBD)
- **Location**: Denver, CO (remote-friendly)

### Ideal Cohort Member
- Passionate about the problem their tech solves
- Very coachable and easy to work with
- Smart, understands business model and market growth
- Fast learner, able to build quickly
- Solo founders and teams both welcome
