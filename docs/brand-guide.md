# ID345 Accelerator - Brand Guide

**Tagline**: We build together.
**Positioning**: Colorado's First AI Accelerator
**Location**: Denver, CO

## Brand Foundation

**Purpose**: Democratize startup building in the AI era by turning a community of builders into funded founders.

**Vision**: Every AI builder in Colorado has a launchpad to turn their ideas into real companies.

**Mission**: ID345 Accelerator takes passionate builders from our community - including people who've never coded before - and gives them the mentorship, structure, and support to launch AI startups in 6 weeks.

**Values**:
1. **Community First**: Everything we do starts with the community
2. **Build > Talk**: Ship real products, not pitch decks
3. **Inclusive by Default**: Anyone who can build deserves a seat
4. **Honest Mentorship**: Direct, caring feedback that makes founders better

**Personality**: Warm, authentic, builder-credible, encouraging

### Visual Effects (Balanced Retro-Modern)

- Scan lines as decorative borders between sections
- Typing animation on hero headline

- Warm amber hover glow on buttons and interactive elements
- Gentle CRT screen curvature on hero imagery
- Section dividers with retro dot/line patterns

## Brand Voice

### Characteristics
- **Warm**: Like a mentor inviting you into the workshop
- **Encouraging**: Everyone belongs, everyone can build
- **Authentic**: No corporate speak, no buzzwords
- **Community-first**: "We" not "I", collective not individual

### Tone Guidelines
- **Website/Marketing**: Welcoming, inspiring, inclusive
- **Sponsor Communications**: Professional warmth, clear value
- **Community Updates**: Casual, peer-level, celebratory
- **Founder Support**: Direct, caring, honest

### Key Messages
1. **Primary**: "We build together." - Community-powered acceleration
2. **Positioning**: Colorado's first AI accelerator
3. **Democratization**: Anyone can build in the AI era
4. **Community Origin**: Born from Wednesday night meetups

### Writing Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use "we", speak inclusively, celebrate builders
- DO: Reference the community, the Wednesday meetups, the space
- DON'T: Use jargon, be condescending, gatekeep
- DON'T: Over-promise, use "disrupt", be Silicon Valley cliche
